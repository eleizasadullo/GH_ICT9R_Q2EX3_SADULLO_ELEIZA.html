CREATIVE DESIGN

Creative design is a process that uses both digital and physical art mediums to create representations that can be used for marketing purposes or personal use. Good designs can help you in creating a unique identity, strong impression and build consumer trust. There are 4 types of design, business, enterprise, products, and execution. All important in their own right.
